Inspired from the ethos of Indian Culture, where we have multiple ‘loks’ such as Vayulok – the land for wind, Prithvilok – the land for life, Paatallok – the land of the underworld.

We are Metalok –

the land of Digital

World.

Metalok is an amalgamation of Architecture, Design and Technology. Powered by the next-gen technologies of Artificial Reality (AR), Virtual Reality (VR), Artificial Intelligence (AI), Extended Reality (XR) and Mixed Reality (MR), we are building a new digital world block by block. Metalok envisions to enable businesses and partners to influence the customer experiential journey in the immersive & interactive virtual world. Starting with Metaverse, Digital Twin, Deeptech-Blockchain technologies and Technical Consultation as building blocks, we commit to bestow immersive & interactive experiences through experiential expeditions and memorabilia.  

The brand then diversified to multiple industrial verticals, with a focus on Real Estate – providing three dimensional property experiences. Embracing the new term of Property Tech, Metalok drives– and with success, integration of technology into the real estate experiences of both the clients, agents and property developers.   Advanced technology & undertakings that Metalok implements span over as 3D configurators for space interiors making customization visually appealing, AR brochures, touchscreen modules and Experience centers redefining marketing strategies, VR Property experiences bringing property viewing to the couch, and more in line to merge Metalok’s futuristic vision with Real Estate.
