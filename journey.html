Spiritual Harmony  
The administration of the time, set a positive tone by equally distributing the site to the concerned parties. In 1885, filing the first plea filed over the dispute marked the beginning of the Ram Temple Movement.

1949

Major juncture in the Movement 
Everybody’s beloved Ram Lalla idols were excavated at the Ram Janmabhoomi altering the momentum of the Ram Temple movement. The people of India were saddened by the consequences leading to the locking of the temple premises to the public, allowing only the daily rituals by the priests.  

1949

1986

Rejoice over premises re-opening  
These years saw a crucial saga of the launch of a campaign to construct a temple at the Ram Janmabhoomi And legal petitions filed led to the locks of the mosque being opened to allow the Hindus to pray. The temple premises reopened to public worship establishing a spiritual consonance.

Ram Mandir Foundation
The foundation for the temple construction was laid which was celebrated among the Indians across the globe. 

1989

1990

The Ratha Yatra  
The rath yatra, as a part of the Ram Temple Movement was launched from Somnath to Ayodhya which mobilised masses heralding the construction of Ram Mandir

1990

1992

Unexpected Resonance  
Conscious Initiatives were taken towards reconciliation and peace after the seismic event to revitalize the delicate harmony existing among the Indian communities since centuries, as conceived in the Rama Rajya.

1992

2003

ASI Survey  
ASI, after a thorough excavation of the site collected compelling evidence of the existence of a temple at the site, closing the curtains over a long-standing exchange of perspectives.

2003

2010

Proposal of Equal Distribution  
An Agreement to distribute the land among the social and religious peers of India, as a step towards encouragement in practice of individual and independent faith, promoting mutual respect and understanding.

2010

2011

The Supreme Court Guidance   Astay on the land distribution was issued by the Apex Court for more detailed deliberation upholding the Indian Integrity.

2011

2019

Historic Verdict by Supreme Court  
In this landmark judgement, Supreme court entrusted the land to a trust established by the Government, to oversee the construction of the Ram Mandir.

2019

2020

Foundation Stone of Ram Mandir  
The Prime Minister of India laid the foundation stone on August, 5th 2020, marking the commencement of the temple construction. A commemorative plaque was also unveiled along with the release of a special postal stamp on this momentous occasion.

2020

2024

TOn January 22, 2024, the Pran Pratishtha of Lord Rama in the avatar of Ram Lalla occurred in accordance with Vedic rituals marking a momentous event of spiritual and cultural significance and progress to India. The Ram Mandir now stands as an epitome of Unity and Diversity in alignment with India’s national ethos.
